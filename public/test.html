The Origin of Burgie (NSFW)

Know Your Meme: Burgie

Fun Fact: The name of my Heroku repo for this app came from a coworker who received a text message with an amusing autocorrect. Someone from her church meant to text her about a "Prayer Request" but it came across as "Prairie Quest" instead. She couldn't stop laughing about it and I thought it sounded like a name Heroku would generate, so prairie-quest-01010 was born!
